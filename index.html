FAQ

Common Questions

Is Clara really free?

Yes! Clara is completely free to use. We believe everyone deserves smart scheduling tools, not just those who can afford premium subscriptions.

Which calendars does Clara support?

Clara integrates with Google Calendar, Microsoft Outlook, and Apple Calendar. You can connect multiple accounts from each provider.

How does the health integration work?

Clara connects to Apple Health to read your sleep, steps, and heart rate variability. This data helps Clara understand your energy levels and make better scheduling suggestions.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We use OAuth for calendar connections (we never see your passwords), and you can delete your data at any time.

Does Clara work offline?

Yes! Your calendar is cached locally, so you can view your schedule offline. Changes sync automatically when you're back online.

Is there an Android version?

Clara is currently iOS-only, but we're working on Android. Join our waitlist to be notified when it's available.
